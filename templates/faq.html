General Questions

1. How do I book a flight on FlyNext?

To book a flight on FlyNext, simply enter your departure and destination cities, select your travel dates, and choose from the available flights. Follow the prompts to enter your passenger details and complete the payment process.

2. How will I receive my e-ticket?

After completing your booking, your e-ticket will be sent to the email address you provided during the booking process.

3. What payment methods are accepted on FlyNext?

FlyNext accepts major credit and debit cards. All payments are securely processed through our website.

4. What should I do if I didn’t receive my e-ticket?

If you haven't received your e-ticket, please check your spam or junk mail folder. If it's still not there, contact our customer support team for assistance.

Booking Related

1. Can I book a flight for someone else using my account?

Yes, you can book a flight for someone else using your FlyNext account. Simply enter the passenger details of the person you’re booking for during the reservation process.

2. Can I change or cancel my flight after booking?

Yes, you can change or cancel your flight after booking. Please contact the customer support for assistance. Note that changes and cancellations may be subject to fees as per the airline's policy..

3. Can I book multiple flights in one transaction on FlyNext?

No, FlyNext only allows you to book one flight per transaction. If you need to book additional flights, you will need to complete separate bookings for each flight.

4. Can I hold a reservation on FlyNext and pay later?

No, FlyNext does not offer the option to hold a reservation and pay later. All bookings must be paid in full at the time of reservation to secure your flight.
